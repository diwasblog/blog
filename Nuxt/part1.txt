Hosted Platform and Self Hosting Platform

Self Hosting Websites :
Self hosting is when you pay a webhost company like bluehost, agmhosting to host your sites on the internet.
Here you own your own website and you have unlimited capabilities to do with it.

Here, Hosting Platform : Bluehost, Agmhosting, Wordpress.org




Non Self Hosting Website
It is one that you have not paid for , one that you host on 'free' website platform such as Tumblr, Blogger,
Wix , wordpress.com or some other non paid host.

Here you have limited capability to do, you cannot monetize it.


Importance of self hosted website:
1)Full Ownership and unlimited capabilities
2)A self hosting website is professional
3)Can monetize
