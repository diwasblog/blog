Backlinks

Links of your sites to other sites.

Types of Backlinks

1)Inbound(Incoming) Backlinks

If other sites put your sites url in their sites , then it will be your incoming backlinks.

I have my links in wikipedia, so it is incoming links for me.

 

2)Outbound( Outgoing) Backlinks

If you creates other sites url in your sites, then it is outgoing backlinks.

If we links wikipedia in our sites article then it is outbound links.

 

No Follow and doFollow Links

Note: Google do not crawl nofollow links.

Nofollow links will looks like this:

<a href="..."   rel="noFollow">Flag of Nepal</a>

<a href="..."   rel="external noFollow">Flag of Nepal</a>
