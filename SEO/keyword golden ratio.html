KGR (Keyword Golden Ratio) = (#of allintitle search) / ( total search volume)

less than 0.25 0.25 - 1.00 1.00  greater
Good Might Work Bad

If less than 0.25 then if you write the article on allintitle topic then when google indexed the article then it will be 100% sure that your content will rank within 50 .

 

Eg. allintitle: best technology stack

  • Allintitle total search => 250
  • Total Searches  ==> 1000

Then KGR is  250/1000 = 0.25

If KGR is less than 0.25 then you should rank in the top 50 soon.

Why only 250 ?

  • Search Volume = 10000 . If KGR is 0.25 that means you have 2500 other sites.
  • KGR volume  under 0.25 and search volume under 250, then you are only competing with a maximum of 62.5
