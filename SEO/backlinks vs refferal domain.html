Backlinks vs Referal Domain

Backlinks: Links of your sites in another site, so you get traffics.Often we don't link homepage link as a backlink.

Referral Domain: Domains where the backlinks are coming from.

Suppose you have set www.ourtechroom/tech/hackingpc in another site then this full urlstring is 1 backlinks. 

Here referral Domain is www.ourtechroom.com and referral domains counts as 1

For instance, if you have 10,000 backlinks from 200 sites, it means you have 200 referring domains. 

Warning: Google can penalty you if you create many backlinks in one site.

Important

We almost always see more backlinks than referring domains. Impossible to have more referring domains than backlinks.

If we have 100 backlinks and they come 50 domains, we have a ratio of:

2:1 (2 backlinks per 1 referring domain)

Ideally, we want this number to be pretty close to 1:1 to maximise our SEO, time, and effort.
