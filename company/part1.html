How can I control students from talking in the class?

 Break your whole session into sub sessions of 10-15 mins each. As that's the average attention span of kids.

Make your sessions inter active. Force them to participate. Repeatedly ask questions. Encourage answering even if wrong.

Between the sub sessions either diverge the topic into something fun or play a little game of 2-3 mins. 

Make interactive via video or image.

Last but not the least don't be authoritative unless & until it's strictly about discipline. Let them have a bit of chit chat and fun.Teacher can speak to their students as calm and politely.
