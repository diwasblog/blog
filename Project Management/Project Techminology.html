WBS (Work BreakDown Structure )

The WBS is a method for getting a complex, multi-step project done. It's a way to divide and conquer large projects so you can get things done faster and more efficiently.

It is a process of breaking a large and complex process in simpler and manageable hierarchical tasks.

Some Characteristics:

  • Definable: can be described and easily understood by project participants.
  • Manageable: a meaningful unit of work where specific responsibility and authority can be assigned to a responsible individual.
  • Estimateable : Duration to complete a part can be estimated.
  • Measurable: Can be used to measure progress.
  • Adaptable: Addition and removal of a part can be easily adopted.

MileStone ( diamond shape)

If life was a project then measure milestone would be get a married, buying a house , and having a kid.

Milestone is a landmark movement that is necessary for you to achieve in order to move on next step.

If shooting a movie is your project then casting a actor and actress is your major milestone, which is necessary to move forward for making the movie.

 

The milestones should represent a clear sequence of events that incrementally build up until your project is complete. Project milestones are a way of knowing how the project is advancing, especially if you’re not familiar with the tasks being executed. 

 

If building your house is your project then 

  1. The floors will be finished on Monday
  2. The roof will be completed on November 1st
  3. The gas installation will be connected at the end of the month

Think of each of the above as a milestone—or a clear sequence of events that build up until your home/project is complete.

Remittance 

Remittance is a sum of money earned by the citizen of the country(eg. Nepal) working abroad (say korea) and transfered to his / her family residing in the country of the origin (Nepal )

Remittance types:

1) Inward Remittance: Transfer of money from place to another within the national territory.

2)Outward Remittance: Transfer of money from place to another from one country to another.

India is the largest recipient of Remittance amount to 79 billion dollars in 2018, (world bank )
