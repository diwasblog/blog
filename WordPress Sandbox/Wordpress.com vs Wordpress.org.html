WordPress.com vs WordPress.org ? Which is Better?

If you are a personal blogger, and you don't care about making money from your website ,then go with free WordPress.com

If you are a business or a blogger that wants to make money from your site, then we recommend using the self-hosted WordPress.org. It gives you the freedom and flexibility to grow your website the way you want.That’s the platform that every professional blogger, small business owner, and even big name brands likes Disney uses.

To start a self-hosted WordPress website, you need a domain name and WordPress hosting.

A domain name is your website’s address on the internet such as google.com, wpbeginner.com, etc. Web hosting is where your website files are stored on the internet.
