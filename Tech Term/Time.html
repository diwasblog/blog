Date and Time Concept

Earth: 360 degree

Let's divide it into 24 parts so, each part has 360/24 = 15 degrees.

So, we have 24 imaginary lines drawn on earth . At zero degree we have Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) or Prime Meridian

If we move towards the east of GMT  by one imaginary line we get 1 hours plus. So, positive offset towards east.

If we move towards the west of GMT by one imaginary line we get 1 hour minus.So ,negative offset towards west.

So, officially we have 24 timezones but we have more than 24 timezones maintained .Two or Three Time zones within US , they have done this on the basis of their border states or else.

 

Why this concept ?

This concept is based on the rotation of the earth.
