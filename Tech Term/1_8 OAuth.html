What is OAuth ?

Some says OAuth as login flow (like when you sign into an application with Google Login) and some says its "Security Thing " .But it is not so.

OAuth is not an API or a service: it’s an open standard for authorization and anyone can implement it.

Specially, OAuth is a standard that apps can use to provide client applications with “secure delegated access”.

OAuth works over HTTPS and authorizes devices, APIs, servers, and applications with access tokens rather than credentials(ie. username and password ).

It has two version

1)OAuth 1.0 

2)OAuth 2.0

OAuth 2.0 is the most widely used form of OAuth
