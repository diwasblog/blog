Bitcoin

It is a secured decentralized payment currency.It is in digital form. 

In the current process for amount transaction between two parties, we need third party entities and that third parties entity can be a bank. Suppose A wants to transfer money to B, then A has to go to bank and pay a certain charges to bank and bank will transfer that money to intended account ie to the account of B. But in bitcoin we don't need third parties , directly A can send digital currency (not physical currency) to B with no any broker in between them . And this whole system is handled by Blockchain Technology.
