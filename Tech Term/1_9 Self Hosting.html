Self Hosting ?

Web Hosting means to deploy your project to a web server, usually IIS.

When Self hosting, you create a host ny yourself, usually a Windows Service. Both approaches are different. While you can use the web servers infrastructure and admin tools for the earlier, you need to provide some of these yourself when Self Hosting. On the other hand, you can tailor configuration and deployment of your service to the requirements and background of your users.
