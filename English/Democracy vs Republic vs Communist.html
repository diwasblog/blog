Democracy(लोकतान्त्रिक) vs Republic(गणतन्त्र)

Democracy System: In simple words, "by the people, to the people, for the people" . Here, people elect the representation via the voting process and the majority of people representation will be in the government. There is nothing like the constitution of a set of rules. Whatever the majority says is the rule. So, lower label people can be representative. Example: Japan

Republic System: Here, there will be the law and everyone should follow that law. It's like this "by the people, of the people, for the people but following a set of rules" in that case our constitution.  Here, lower label people cannot be representative but higher label people can be representative.

Nepal has become the federal democratic republic country in 15th Jestha, 2065.

So we conclude:

Till Election: Democracy

After Election: Republic

 

Federal(संघीय) System:  A federal government is a system of dividing up power between a central national government and local state governments that are connected to one another by the national government.

Absolute Monarchy(राजतन्त्र) System :All power is in the hand of a single person. "The king". Example: Oman, Qatar, Brunei (Sultan), UAE

Constitutional Monarchy(संवैधानिक राजतन्त्र) :Best example is United Kingdom. where the King or Queen is the Head of the state.....just for namesake. They have some reserve powers, but the main decision making lies with elected members.

Capitalism vs Communism

Capitalism (पूंजीवादी ) System :  Here, rich people or capitalist or who accumulate a lot of wealth influence the decision making of govt. and policies so as to increase the personal profit and accumulate more wealth. Example : Cuba, North Korea & Zimbabwe.

Here, people can make product and establish the company.

 

Communism(साम्यवाद ) System :Here, government has full power and authority and no local people can establish company .All have to work under government companies. No one can establish companies but only the government can do.

DictatorShip(तानाशाही) vs Monarchy

A dictatorship is an office that has been gotten through force, and a monarchy  is reign that is passed from one generation to another.


For example, Saddam Hussein was the dictator of Iraq till the U.S. forces killed him. Benito Mussolini was also a dictator who ruled Italy from 1925 to 1943.
