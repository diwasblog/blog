Undergraduate(UG) Student

  • Suppose you are doing/pursuing a bachelor degree and have not passed out / haven't got a certificate then he/she is an undergraduate student.
  • They have completed a higher secondary degree already

Graduate Student

  • That student who has passed out and got a bachelor's degree certificate then he/she is a graduate student.
  • Bachelor is the mostly used prefix for Graduates.
  • Eg. Bachelor of Applied Science (BAS), Bachelor of Science (B.S.)

Postgraduate(PG) Student

  • That student who has completed master degree then he/she is a postgraduate students.
  • Post-graduation can only be done after graduation.
  • Master is the most used prefix for postgraduates
  • master's/postgraduate degree

Doctoral Student

After master degree  we have doctorial degree / phd degree.

 

Associate's degrees

  • They are designed for students who wish to obtain training in a specific area, such as nursing or mechanics, without obtaining a 4-year degree. 
  • Associate of Arts (A.A.) , Associate of Applied Science (AAS), Associate of Science (A.S.)
  • Generally 2 years program
