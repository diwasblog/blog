OATS 

  • Has Large quantity of Fibre
  • Less Fats
  • Has no glutin
  • Has Vitamin and Minerals
  • Has large Calorie  ie. 100gm oats == > 390 kcal ( So, oats is not  good for fatty person . So , if you want to loss weight then donot take OATS in more amount)

General 3 types of OATS:

1) Rolled OATS :

  • least-processed oats.
  •  high in protein and fiber. 
  • take up to an hour to cook( good for slow cooker). Soaking them overnight cuts cooking time in half. 

2) Steel OATS

3) Instant OATS

  • Thinner and fragmented than Rolled and Steel OATS.
  • allows water to be absorbed much faster during cooking.
  • High vitamins and fiber but lesser nutrients than Steel and OATS.
