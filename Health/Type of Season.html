Types of season ( in foreign land) each of 4 month

Spring(वसन्त) :  During this time, trees begin to develop (bud) and flowers start to bloom. Rainy days make it easy for plants, flowers and grass to grow.

Autumn (शरद ) : Known as fall.  It brings cold temperature and frost. Animals prepare for hibernation during this season by eating a lot as they need to save the fat and energy for the harsh winter months.

Winter ( जाडो ) :  Snow and Ice

Summer : hot 

 

Six season in Nepal ( 2 month each)

  1. Basanta Ritu  (Spring): Cold Start to ends
  2. Grishma Ritu (Summer) : Garmi huna thalxa. Fruits are mostly grown here.
  3. Barsha Ritu (Rainy) :rains
  4. Sharad Ritu (Autumn) : rains starts to stop 
  5. Hemanta Ritu (Pre-winter) : After Dashain
  6. Shishir Ritu (Winter) : Magh - Flagun
