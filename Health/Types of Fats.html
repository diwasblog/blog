Good Fats

 Good but recommended to reduce the intake of saturated fats. They are unsaturated (good) fats which have been partially saturated with hydrogen to extend their shelf life. Eg. Fried Chicken, Crackers etc.

Cholesterol

Cholesterol is only made from Saturated Fats Products. ie. From Animal Product.

Eg. Cheese, Butter, Milk, Ghee, Meat, etc. contains cholesterol

       Fruits and Vegetables do not contain cholesterol.

Tryglyceride (Types of Oil)

Plants, as well as Animal products, contain tryglyceride.

Tryglycerides are bad for heart.
