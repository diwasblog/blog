Hypoglycaemia (low blood sugar)

low level of glucose in your blood.

Signs and symptoms of hypoglycaemia can include:

  • feeling hungry
  • sweating
  • dizziness
  • tiredness (fatigue)
  • blurred vision
  • trembling or shakiness
  • going pale
  • fast pulse or palpitations
  • tingling lips
  • irritability
  • difficulty concentrating
  • confusion
  • disorderly or irrational behaviour, which may be mistaken for drunkenness

PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction)

It is a technique that is used to make several copies of a small fragment of DNA /RNA.

Use in :

  • Diagnosing Infection
  • Genetic Research
  • Crime Investigation
