Beam   Lintel
The beam carries the load from the slab/floor/roof and transfers it to the column. From column, it is transferred to the footing, and from footing finally to the soil.   Lintel carries the load of the wall above the openings/door/window, and transfer it to the walls only.
It’s end point is rested on columns   It’s end point is rested on walls
It does not contain door and windows under it   It contains door and windows under it

Types of BEAM 

Those which are present below ground we call it Sub Structure and Above the group, we call it Super Structure.

Plinth beam

  • First BEAM above the ground level is the Plinth beam. It is between SubStructure and superstructure.
  • Main Works of Plinth Beam is the transfer of load from brickwork to pillar /column to the foundation(substructure). Also allow connection between column to column.

DPC (damp-proof course )

  • Above that, we have DPC, between DPC and Plinth Beam we add brick to increase the height of the building.
  • The main work of DPC is the flowing of water from the group above DPC via capillary reaction.

Tie Beam

  • Lentil Level Beam we call it Tie Beam.
  • It function is to transfer the load present above the door and windows to the column and then to the ground

Floor Beam

Floor Beam is used at the top. It transfers SLAB load to column and column to ground.
